Air Fryer Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs are good for you - make your life easier by using your airfryer to make the perfect scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Lightly coat a ramekin dish with the cooking spray.
  2. Pour in beaten eggs.
  3. Set airfryer to 180 degrees celcius and place the dish into the air fryer basket.
  4. Air fry for 3 minutes. Stir the egg mixture and cook for an additional 3 minutes. Fluff the eggs with a fork and season to taste with salt and pepper.
